What is your square-off charges?

When you place an intraday order and the system squares off your position, you pay a nominal fee. You will be levied auto square-off charges of ₹50 (plus GST of 18%) per order for open positions squared-off by the RMS team at the usual intraday square-off times. To avoid auto square-off charges, you can square off your positions yourself through mobile app or web before 3:15 pm.
